Mumbai - The City of Dreams

Mumbai, the "City of Dreams," is India's financial capital and a vibrant metropolis known for its bustling streets, colonial architecture, and Bollywood glamour.

Top Attractions

  • Gateway of India – Iconic waterfront monument.
  • Marine Drive – Scenic coastal road perfect for evening strolls.
  • Elephanta Caves – Ancient rock-cut temples on Elephanta Island.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus – A UNESCO-listed railway station.
  • Colaba Causeway – A shopper’s paradise for souvenirs and fashion.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: November to February.
  • Local transport: Mumbai local trains, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.
  • Must-try street food: Vada pav, pav bhaji, and bhel puri.
