Sun Temple

Discover the ancient Sun Temple, renowned for its architectural brilliance and historical significance.

The Great History of Kedarnath:-

  • Built in the 13th century by King Narasimhadeva I of the Eastern Ganga Dynasty.
  • Designed as a chariot of the Sun God, Surya, featuring 12 intricately carved stone wheels and 7 stone horses.
  • Originally constructed near the Chandrabhaga River, but the coastline has since receded.It is believed that the temple was built to commemorate a military victory and as an offering to the Sun God.
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1984, recognized for its architectural brilliance.
